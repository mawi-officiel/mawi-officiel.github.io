Snack Online — Your Smart Platform for Professional Snack Management

In a world full of competition, it has become impossible for any Moroccan snack business to succeed without professional tools that help organize its operations, increase sales, and strengthen its online presence.

That’s where Snack Online comes in — the system that completely changes the rules of the game.

It’s not just a website — it’s a smart, all-in-one system that includes everything you need to manage, develop, and market your snack business easily and efficiently — with no technical experience required.

Full control over every detail of your website.

arrow_and_edge

From the Snack Online dashboard, you have complete control over every part of your website:

Menu Management: Add, edit, or delete meals whenever you want. You can easily set the menu title and image.

Meals and Special Offers Management: Add, edit, or delete meals whenever you want. You can easily set the price, description, image, and offers, and create seasonal meals, discounts, or special menus for events.

Complete Website Settings: You can change your logo, page texts, and more — all from a single, simple dashboard that delivers professional performance.

Everything on the website can be controlled and customized with just one click.

Team Collaboration System — Professional Coordination for Teamwork

No project can succeed without proper team organization — that’s why Snack Online comes with an advanced collaboration system that lets you manage your staff with ease:

Flexible permissions — Create dedicated accounts for your staff (orders management, menu management, meals management, support, etc.) and assign precise access levels to each one of them.

Live task distribution system — The platform operates in real time, displaying all updates the moment they occur. This prevents any conflicts or overlaps in modifications between team members, allowing everyone to work simultaneously with complete smoothness and zero task conflicts.

Instant notifications — Each team member receives alerts whenever a new order arrives or its status changes.

The result: a fully synchronized team that operates like a single machine — organized, fast, and precise.

  • Helps auto-optimize campaigns.
  • Sends automated reminders to recover abandoned carts.
  • Lets you track the customer journey end-to-end.

Google Analytics
