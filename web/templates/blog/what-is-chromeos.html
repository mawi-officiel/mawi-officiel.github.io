What is the Chrome OS Operating System?

An OS built for the web, featuring speed, simplicity, and security. Perfect for students and users who rely on cloud services.

cloud

Defining the System

Chrome OS is a lightweight OS developed by Google, based primarily on the Chrome browser. Designed to be fast-booting, virus-secure, and easy to manage.

  • boltSpeed: Boots in seconds and doesn't slow down over time.
  • appsApps: Supports Android, Linux, and Web apps.
  • cloud_doneCloud: Everything is auto-saved to Google Drive.
